## 1. Subject

The Author grants the Recipient access to the Data **solely for the purpose of evaluating language models**.

The Data comprises the test items, their structure, and their keys. Access to the Data confers no rights to the method by which the Data was constructed, and no rights to the scoring procedure.

## 2. Prohibited uses

The Recipient may not:

1. **redistribute** the Data or any part of it, publicly or privately, whole or in fragments;
2. **include the Data in any training corpus, fine-tuning set, or retrieval store** of any model;
3. **publish the items**, or any reconstruction of them, including paraphrases and examples derived from real items;
4. **reproduce or reverse-engineer** the procedure by which grammatical correctness is adjudicated;
5. **transfer access** to third parties without the prior written consent of the Author.

## 3. Permitted use

The Recipient may run the Data against models under its own control for the purpose of measurement, and may **publish numerical results** — score, run identifier, date, model version — provided that no items are disclosed.

## 5. Canary

The Data contains a unique canary identifier. The Recipient must not remove or mask it. The appearance of this identifier in a model's training data is treated as a breach of §2.2.

## 6. Term and breach

The obligations under §2 apply without time limit.

Breach results in immediate revocation of access. The Author reserves all remedies available under applicable law.
